About

Łukasz is a juggler from the southern Poland. He started practising in 2010 and has been passionated about juggling ever since.

Although being mostly self-taught, he has participated in various workshops during juggling conventions around Europe and on a course in the University of Dance and Circus in Stockholm. He tries to push the limits of what is possible in juggling.

Łukasz's shows and videos have been seen by more than half a million people.
